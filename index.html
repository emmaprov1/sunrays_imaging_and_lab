Who we are

SunRays Imaging & Laboratory services Ltd, is an indigenous company registered in Nigeria to help alleviate health care needs of the masses in the areas of Medical Imaging and Medical laboratory. The aim of establishing the company is not only to positively impact on the lives of Nigerians, in the area of healthcare but to also impact on Nigerian economy through the provision of healthcare services that can help reduce the burden of medical tourism on the economy.
