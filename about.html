Welcome to SunRays Imaging & Laboratory Services Ltd

SunRays Imaging & Laboratory services Ltd, is an indigenous company registered in Nigeria to help alleviate health care needs of the masses in the areas of Medical Imaging and Medical laboratory. The aim of establishing the company is not only to positively impact on the lives of Nigerians, in the area of healthcare but to also impact on Nigerian economy through the provision of healthcare services that can help reduce the burden of medical tourism on the economy. Furthermore, to also create a positive impact in the growth of Nigerian economy through employment of best Nigerian brains, either from the country or internationally, to offer their expert services to the masses.

We provide expert imaging and medical laboratory services to patients/customers in need of them, and we have highly trained and dedicated staffs that are always available and willing to serve our customers in their various areas of need to the end.

As a functioning company, we understand the challenges many business similar to us encounter on a daily bases. In order to jump over these hurdles and ensure seamless flow of our service delivery, we have created a sale and supply unit of the company to deal with procurement and supply of radiography/medical equipments and installation, not just for our use but for supply to other company or similar bussiness outfit in need of them.

Our Mission

As our motto “in service to humanity” states, we are here simply to serve you through the provision of quality and efficient healthcare services, that will help alleviate the pains of ill health and if possible, therapeutically render treatment to some of them.

Our Goal

To promote good health and general well being in our customers

Our Vision

To be the best health care service Provider in Nigeria.
